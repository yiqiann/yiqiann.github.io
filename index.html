Prevalence of Mental Health Disorders Across The World

Mental health disorder refers to a clinically significant disturbance in how an individual thinks, behaves or manages their emotions (World Health Organisation, 2022). This is usually closely related with factors like emotional distress. This visualisation will cover seven types of mental health disorders, which are Schizophrenia, Bipolar Disorder, Disorder, Anxiety Disorder, Substance Use Disorder, Depression and Alcohol Use Disorder.
